Cambridge tip: the three most tested laws are aᵐ × aⁿ = aᵐ⁺ⁿ, aᵐ ÷ aⁿ = aᵐ⁻ⁿ, and (aᵐ)ⁿ = aᵐⁿ. Always write a⁰ = 1 and a⁻ᵐ = 1/aᵐ without a calculator. Fractional indices: a^(m/n) = (ⁿ√a)ᵐ — take the root first, then the power, to keep numbers small.
