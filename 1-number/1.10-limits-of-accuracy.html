Cambridge tip: LB = value − half the accuracy, UB = value + half the accuracy. UB is always excluded (strict inequality, open circle). For products: max = UB₁ × UB₂; for quotients: max = UB₁ ÷ LB₂. Always state the inequality as LB ≤ x < UB.
