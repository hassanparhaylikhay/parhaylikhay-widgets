Cambridge tip: leading zeros are NOT significant figures — 0.0074 has 2 SF, not 4. For estimation questions, round each value to 1 SF first, then calculate — this earns M1. Write the estimate clearly; do not just write the exact answer. Trailing zeros after a decimal point ARE significant (3.40 has 3 SF).
