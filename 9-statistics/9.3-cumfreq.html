Cumulative Frequency — Median & Quartiles from the S-Curve
Adjust each class's frequency. The S-curve updates live. Horizontal lines at n/4, n/2, 3n/4 automatically read off Q1, median, and Q3 from the curve.
Cambridge tip: plot cumulative frequency at the upper class boundary, not the midpoint. Read off the value for n/2 to get the median. IQR = Q3 − Q1. Always start your curve from (lower boundary of first class, 0).
