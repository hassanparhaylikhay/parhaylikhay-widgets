Cambridge tip: CAST — the quadrant rule: Q1 (0°–90°): all positive. Q2 (90°–180°): only sin positive. Q3 (180°–270°): only tan positive. Q4 (270°–360°): only cos positive. Cambridge questions sometimes give an obtuse angle — you must know sin is still positive above 90°.
