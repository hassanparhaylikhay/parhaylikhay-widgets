Cambridge tip: both formulas are printed on the paper — arc = \(\frac{\theta}{360} \times 2\pi r\) and area = \(\frac{\theta}{360} \times \pi r^2\). You still need to show the substitution step (e.g. \(\frac{120}{360} \times 2\pi \times 5\)) before simplifying — that line earns the M1 method mark.
