Cambridge tip: always measure clockwise from North — write the full three figures (e.g. 045°, not 45°). Back-bearing = forward bearing ± 180°. Add 180° if bearing < 180°, subtract if ≥ 180°. Never use compass letters (NE, SW) in your answer — examiners want the three-figure number.
