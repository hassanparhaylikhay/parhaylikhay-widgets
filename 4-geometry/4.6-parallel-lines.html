Drag the transversal angle handle to change θ — see which angles are equal and which are supplementary.
Cambridge tip: always state the angle rule by name — "alternate angles" or "corresponding angles" earns the reasoning mark (R1). Co-interior (same-side interior) angles add up to 180° — they are supplementary, not equal. You need both the answer AND the reason to get full marks.
