Vectors — Head-to-Tail Addition & Subtraction
Drag the tips of a and b (and their tails). The resultant vector shows a+b or a−b. Switch modes to see how subtraction reverses b and adds it head-to-tail.
Cambridge tip: always write vectors as column vectors in Cambridge 4024 — not as (x, y) coordinates. The magnitude of vector v = xy is |v| = √(x² + y²). To go from A to B using position vectors: AB = b − a.
