Reflection — Mirror Line Explorer
Drag the two yellow handles to set any mirror line you like. Watch the perpendicular bisectors confirm each vertex maps exactly to its image. Use the preset buttons for the lines Cambridge tests most.
Cambridge tip: always state the mirror line as an equation — "reflect in y = x" earns the mark, "reflect diagonally" does not. For y = x: swap the coordinates — point (a, b) maps to (b, a). For x-axis: (a, b) → (a, −b). For y-axis: (a, b) → (−a, b).
